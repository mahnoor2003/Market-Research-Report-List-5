<p><p>DNA sequencing is a process used to determine the order of nucleotides in a DNA molecule. There are different types of sequencing technologies, including Emulsion PCR, Bridge Amplification, and Single-Molecule sequencing. Emulsion PCR involves amplifying millions of DNA fragments in tiny droplets. Bridge Amplification uses DNA templates attached to a solid surface to amplify and sequence DNA. Single-Molecule sequencing directly reads individual DNA strands. These technologies are used in various applications such as medical diagnostics, genetic research, and personalized medicine.</p></p>
