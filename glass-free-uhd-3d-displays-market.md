<p><p>Glass-free UHD 3D Displays come in two main types - Parallax Barrier Technology and Lenticular Display Technology. Parallax Barrier Technology uses a barrier with precisely positioned slits to create a 3D effect by directing different images to each eye. Lenticular Display Technology uses an array of lenses to project multiple views of a scene to different angles, creating a 3D effect without the need for special glasses. Both technologies aim to provide a high-quality, immersive 3D viewing experience without the use of traditional 3D glasses.</p></p>
