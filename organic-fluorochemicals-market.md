<p><p>Organic Fluorochemicals are divided into three main types based on their chemical structure: Aliphatic Compounds, Aromatic Compounds, and Fluorobenzene. Aliphatic Compounds are characterized by straight or branched carbon chains, while Aromatic Compounds contain a ring structure. Fluorobenzene is a type of Aromatic Compound that consists of a benzene ring with fluorine atoms attached. Each type of Fluorochemical has unique properties and applications in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, and surface coatings.</p></p>
