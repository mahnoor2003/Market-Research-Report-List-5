<p><p>Stainless steel gate valves are available in two main types based on their connection method: flanged connection and threaded connection. Flanged connection gate valves are designed to be connected to pipes using flanges, providing a secure and leak-proof connection. Threaded connection gate valves, on the other hand, have threaded ends that allow them to be directly screwed onto pipes. Both types offer secure and reliable control of flow in various industrial applications.</p></p>
