<p><strong>The Electric Well Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Openwell</li><li>Borewell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric well pumps are classified into two main types: Openwell pumps and Borewell pumps. Openwell pumps are designed for use in open wells or tanks, where the water level is close to the surface. They are commonly used in residential and commercial settings for applications such as irrigation and water supply. Borewell pumps, on the other hand, are specifically designed for use in deep borewells where the water level is below ground level. They are commonly used for agricultural, industrial, and municipal water supply purposes.</p></p>
