<p><p>Floating Production Storage and Offloading (FPSO) refers to a type of floating vessel used in the offshore oil and gas industry for production, storage, and offloading operations. There are three main types of FPSO market configurations: Spread Mooring, Single Point Mooring (SPM), and Dynamic Positioning (DP). Spread Mooring uses multiple anchors to keep the FPSO in place, SPM uses a single anchor point, and DP uses thrusters to constantly adjust the position of the FPSO. Each configuration has unique advantages and is chosen based on specific project requirements.</p></p>
