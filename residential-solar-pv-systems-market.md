<p><p>Residential Solar PV Systems market can be divided into two main types: Organic PV and Inorganic PV. Organic PV systems use carbon-based materials in their solar cells, offering flexibility and low-cost manufacturing. In contrast, Inorganic PV systems use materials like silicon, cadmium telluride, or copper indium gallium selenide, providing higher efficiency and longer lifespans. Both types are utilized in residential settings to convert sunlight into electricity, reducing reliance on traditional energy sources and lowering carbon emissions.</p></p>
