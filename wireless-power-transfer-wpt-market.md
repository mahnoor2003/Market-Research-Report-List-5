<p><strong>The Wireless Power Transfer (WPT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near-Field Power Transfer</li><li>Far-Field Power Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless Power Transfer (WPT) technology enables the transmission of power without the need for physical connections. Near-Field Power Transfer involves short-range communication between the transmitter and receiver, generally up to a few centimeters. This type is used for charging devices like smartphones and wearables in close proximity. Far-Field Power Transfer, on the other hand, relies on longer-range communication, allowing for power transmission over larger distances, typically used in applications such as electric vehicle charging and powering remote sensors.</p></p>
