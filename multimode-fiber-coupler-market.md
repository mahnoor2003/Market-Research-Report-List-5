<p><p>Multimode fiber couplers are available in various configurations, including 1×2, 1×4, 1×8, and 2×2. A 1×2 coupler splits the input signal into two output ports, while a 1×4 coupler splits the signal into four ports, and a 1×8 splits it into eight ports. A 2×2 coupler combines two input signals into two output ports. Each type of coupler is designed for specific applications that require the splitting or combining of optical signals in multimode fiber networks.</p></p>
