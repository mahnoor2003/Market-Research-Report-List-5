<p><p>A hybrid boiler is a type of heating system that combines a traditional boiler with a renewable energy source such as an air source heat pump or a ground source heat pump. The boiler with air source heat pump type utilizes the heat from the outside air to supplement the heating system. The boiler with ground source heat pump type extracts heat from the ground to provide additional heating. Other types of hybrid boilers may combine the traditional boiler with another form of renewable energy source for greater energy efficiency.</p></p>
