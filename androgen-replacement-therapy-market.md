<p><p>Androgen Replacement Therapy (ART) is a treatment option for individuals with low levels of androgens, such as testosterone. This therapy is commonly used to treat conditions such as hypogonadism, delayed puberty, androgen deficiency in aging males, and certain types of breast cancer.</p><p>The global Androgen Replacement Therapy Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.20% during the forecast period. The market is also witnessing a trend towards the development of novel delivery methods for ART, such as transdermal patches, gels, and implants, which offer improved convenience and efficacy for patients.</p><p>Additionally, the growing geriatric population worldwide, coupled with the rising incidence of age-related hormonal imbalances, is further driving the demand for androgen replacement therapy.
