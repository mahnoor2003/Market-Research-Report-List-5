<p><p>Umbilical catheters are medical devices used in neonatal care to access the umbilical vessels for administration of fluids or medications. There are three main types of umbilical catheters: Single-Lumen, Dual-Lumen, and Triple-Lumen. Single-Lumen catheters have one tube for fluid delivery, while Dual-Lumen catheters have two tubes for simultaneous administration of fluids and medications. Triple-Lumen catheters have three tubes which allow for additional functions such as blood sampling. Each type offers unique benefits and is used based on the specific medical needs of the patient.</p></p>
