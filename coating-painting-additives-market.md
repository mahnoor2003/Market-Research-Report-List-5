<p><p>Coating additives market includes various types such as dispersing agents, anti-foam agents, rheology control agents, leveling agents, adhesion promoters, and others. Dispersing agents help in dispersing solids in liquid coatings. Anti-foam agents prevent the formation of foam during the application of coatings. Rheology control agents affect the flow and consistency of the coating. Leveling agents help in achieving a smooth and uniform coating surface. Adhesion promoters enhance the bonding between the substrate and the coating. Other additives serve different purposes in improving the overall performance of the coating.</p></p>
