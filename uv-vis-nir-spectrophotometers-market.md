<p><p>UV-VIS-NIR Spectrophotometers can be classified into two main types: Single Monochromator and Double Monochromator. Single Monochromator spectrophotometers use one monochromator to isolate a specific wavelength of light for analysis, making them simpler and less expensive. On the other hand, Double Monochromator spectrophotometers utilize two monochromators to improve resolution and reduce stray light, resulting in higher accuracy and sensitivity. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the analysis.</p></p>
