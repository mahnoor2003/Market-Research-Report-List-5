<p><ul><li>Kinesthetic Feedback System</li><li>Cutaneous Feedback System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Haptic feedback surgical systems can be categorized into two main types: Kinesthetic Feedback Systems and Cutaneous Feedback Systems. Kinesthetic Feedback Systems provide force and torque feedback to the surgeon's hands, allowing them to feel the resistance and pressure during a procedure. Cutaneous Feedback Systems provide tactile sensations to the surgeon's skin, allowing them to feel the texture and shape of tissues being manipulated. Both types of feedback systems enhance the surgeon's dexterity and precision during minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
