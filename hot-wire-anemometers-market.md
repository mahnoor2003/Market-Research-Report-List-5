<p><p>Hot-Wire Anemometers are devices used to measure air flow velocity. There are mainly two types in the market - Side Heating Type and Direct Heating Type. Side Heating Type anemometers have the heating element placed at the side of the wire, while Direct Heating Type anemometers have the heating element directly on the wire. Side Heating Type anemometers are typically more sensitive and have a faster response time, while Direct Heating Type anemometers are known for their simplicity and durability. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications based on the requirements.</p></p>
