<p><p>The Car Digital Video Recorder (DVR) market is divided into two main types: single-channel and multi-channel DVRs. Single-channel DVRs capture footage from one camera, typically positioned at the front of the vehicle. Multi-channel DVRs have the capability to record footage from multiple cameras simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive view of the vehicle surroundings. Single-channel DVRs are suitable for individual vehicles, while multi-channel DVRs are often used in commercial fleets or for added security in high-risk environments.</p></p>
