<p><p>Fishing waders come in three main types: hip waders, waist-high waders, and chest-high waders. Hip waders are ideal for shallow waters and provide protection up to the hip area. Waist-high waders offer more coverage, extending up to the waist, and are suitable for deeper waters. Chest-high waders are the most versatile, providing full-body coverage up to the chest and allowing anglers to fish in various water depths.
