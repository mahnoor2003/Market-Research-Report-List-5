<p><p>The commercial satellite launch services market consists of different types of orbits, including Low Earth Orbit (LEO), Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), Geostationary Earth Orbit (GEO), and Highly Elliptical Orbit (HEO). LEO satellites orbit close to Earth, providing low latency but requiring more satellites for global coverage. MEO satellites are positioned higher and offer wider coverage for applications like GPS. GEO satellites remain stationary over a specific point on the equator, ideal for communication and weather monitoring. HEO satellites have highly elliptical orbits for specialized missions.</p></p>
