<p><strong>The Automotive Industrial Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Cameras</li><li>Line Scan Cameras</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive industrial camera market consists of three main types: Area Scan Cameras, Line Scan Cameras, and Others. Area Scan Cameras capture a full image in one shot, making them suitable for inspecting moving parts or complex shapes. Line Scan Cameras capture one line of pixels at a time, making them ideal for inspecting continuously moving objects like conveyor belts. The Others category includes specialized cameras such as thermal cameras or 3D cameras that cater to unique automotive inspection needs. Each type caters to different applications within the automotive industry.</p></p>
