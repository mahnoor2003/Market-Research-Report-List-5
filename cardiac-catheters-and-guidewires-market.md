Hexapolar balloon pacing catheters provide advanced pacing options, and semi-floating electrodes are used for electrical stimulation. Wedge pressure catheters measure pressures in the heart chambers, thermodilution infusion catheters deliver fluids and medications, and other catheters serve various specific purposes in cardiac procedures.</p></p>
