<p><p>Smart Cards Automated Fare Collection Systems are utilized in public transit systems for efficient and streamlined fare collection. There are three main types of markets within this system: Farebox, Ticket Vending Machines (TVM), and Validators. Fareboxes are devices installed on buses that enable passengers to pay their fare with a smart card. TVMs are self-service kiosks located at transit stations where riders can purchase or reload their smart cards. Validators are machines that passengers tap their smart cards on to access the transit system.</p></p>
