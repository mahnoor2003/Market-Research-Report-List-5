<p><p>The manual pipetting gun market consists of two main types: single channel pipettes and multichannel pipettes. Single channel pipettes are used for dispensing liquids one at a time, making them ideal for tasks that require precision and accuracy. Multichannel pipettes, on the other hand, have multiple channels that allow for dispensing multiple samples simultaneously, speeding up the pipetting process. Both types of pipettes are essential tools in laboratory settings for tasks such as transferring liquids, mixing reagents, and preparing samples.</p></p>
