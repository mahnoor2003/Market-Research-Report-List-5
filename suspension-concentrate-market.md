<p><ul><li>Water Suspension</li><li>Oil Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Suspension concentrates are liquid formulations of active ingredients used in agriculture. Water suspension concentrates are made by mixing the active ingredient with water and a surfactant to keep the particles dispersed. Oil suspension concentrates are similar, but the active ingredient is mixed with an oil instead of water. Both types of suspension concentrates are used to make it easier to mix and apply pesticides or herbicides on crops. Water suspensions are more common due to their ease of use and compatibility with other formulations. Oil suspensions are preferred for certain types of crops or in specific circumstances.</p></p>
