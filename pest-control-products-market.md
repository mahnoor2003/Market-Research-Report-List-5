Mechanical control relies on physical methods like traps or barriers to manage pests. Biological control uses natural enemies of pests, such as predators or pathogens, to control their populations. Each type of control has its advantages and disadvantages, and the choice of method often depends on factors like the type of pest and environmental concerns.</p></p>
