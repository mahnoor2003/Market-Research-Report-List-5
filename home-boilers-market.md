<p><p>Home boilers market consist of two main types - Non-condensing Boilers and Condensing Boilers. Non-condensing boilers are older models that release waste gases through a chimney, while condensing boilers are more efficient as they capture and recycle waste heat to increase energy efficiency. Condensing boilers are becoming more popular due to their higher efficiency and environmental benefits, while non-condensing boilers are still in use in older homes and buildings. Both types play a significant role in heating homes and providing hot water.</p></p>
