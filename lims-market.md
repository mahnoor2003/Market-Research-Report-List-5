<p><p>LIMS (Laboratory Information Management System) markets can be classified into three types based on their deployment: On-premise LIMS, Cloud-based LIMS, and Remotely Hosted LIMS. On-premise LIMS refers to software installed and operated on the organization's own servers. Cloud-based LIMS is hosted on a remote server and accessed through the internet. Remotely Hosted LIMS involves a third-party provider hosting the software on their servers. Each type offers varying levels of flexibility, scalability, and maintenance requirements to suit different laboratory needs.</p></p>
