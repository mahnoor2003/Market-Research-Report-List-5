<p><p>Sample Preparation Systems Market Types include staining, paraffin embedding, liquid handling, dilution, incubation, hybridization, filtration, and others. Staining involves adding dyes or chemicals to make certain components of a sample visible under a microscope. Paraffin embedding is the process of embedding samples in paraffin wax for easier slicing and staining. Liquid handling systems automate the process of dispensing liquids accurately. Dilution systems dilute samples to achieve optimal concentrations. Incubation systems provide controlled conditions for samples to react. Hybridization systems facilitate the binding of nucleic acids. Filtration systems separate particles from liquids. Other systems may include sample extraction and purification techniques.</p></p>
