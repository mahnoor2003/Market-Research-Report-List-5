<p><p>Automotive labels market types refer to different methods of applying labels to auto parts and products. Pressure sensitive labels are adhesive and easy to apply. Glue-applied labels use a wet glue to adhere to surfaces. Heat transfer labels use heat to transfer the label onto the product. In-mold labels are embedded into the product during manufacturing. Other types may include sleeve labels, shrink labels, and wrap-around labels. Each type offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the automotive application.</p></p>
