<p><p>Composite Diamond Coatings have gained popularity in various industries due to their exceptional properties. Two main types of these coatings are Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD). CVD coatings are created by chemical reactions at high temperatures, resulting in a high-quality, uniform diamond layer. On the other hand, PVD coatings are created through a physical process, depositing a thin film of diamond onto the substrate. Both types of coatings offer unique advantages and cater to different applications in the market.</p></p>
