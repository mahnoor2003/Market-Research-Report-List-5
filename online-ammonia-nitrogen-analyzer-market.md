<p><p>Online Ammonia Nitrogen Analyzers come in various types such as Ion-selective Electrodes (ISE), Ultraviolet (UV) Absorbance, and Colorimetric analyzers. ISE analyzers measure the concentration of ammonia ions in the sample, UV absorbance analyzers detect ammonia by measuring the absorption of UV light, and colorimetric analyzers use a color change reaction to quantify the amount of ammonia present. Each type of analyzer offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications in monitoring and controlling ammonia nitrogen levels in various industries.</p></p>
