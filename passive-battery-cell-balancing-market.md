<p><p>Passive battery cell balancing is a method used to equalize the voltages of individual cells in a battery pack to ensure optimal performance and longevity. This technique involves the use of passive components such as resistors, capacitors, and diodes to transfer energy between cells without the need for active control electronics. Passive balancing is a cost-effective and efficient solution for balancing battery cells in electric vehicles, energy storage systems, and other applications.</p><p>The passive battery cell balancing market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for electric vehicles and renewable energy storage solutions. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period. Technological advancements in battery management systems, increasing investments in electric vehicle infrastructure, and growing awareness about energy efficiency are driving the growth of the passive battery cell balancing market.</p><p>Key trends in the market include the development of advanced passive balancing techniques, such as shunt resistor balancing and floating balancing, to improve the efficiency and performance of battery packs.
