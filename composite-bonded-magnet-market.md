<p><p>Composite bonded magnet market types include injection molding and pressing molding. </p><p>Injection molding involves melting composite materials and injecting them into a mold to create intricate shapes and sizes. This method is ideal for producing complex and detailed magnets with high precision.</p><p>Pressing molding, on the other hand, involves compacting powdered materials using pressure to form magnets. This process is simpler and more cost-effective for producing magnets in large volumes. Both methods offer unique advantages for different applications within the composite bonded magnet market.</p></p>
