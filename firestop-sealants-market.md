<p><strong>The Firestop Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastomeric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Firestop sealants are essential in preventing the spread of fire through openings in building structures. Two main types are elastomeric and intumescent sealants. Elastomeric sealants are flexible and can accommodate movement in the building, making them ideal for joints and gaps. Intumescent sealants, on the other hand, expand when exposed to high temperatures, forming a heat-resistant barrier. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining fire safety in buildings by preventing the spread of flames and smoke.</p></p>
