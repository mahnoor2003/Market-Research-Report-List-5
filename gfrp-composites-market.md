<p><p>GFRP composites can be categorized into two main types based on the type of plastic used: thermosetting plastic products and thermoplastic plastic products. Thermosetting plastic products are materials that undergo irreversible chemical reactions, becoming hard and rigid once cured. On the other hand, thermoplastic plastic products are materials that become soft and pliable when heated and can be reformed when cooled. Both types of GFRP composites have their own unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
