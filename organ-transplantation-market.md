<p><p>Organ transplantation market includes autograft, allograft, and other types of transplants. Autograft involves transferring tissues from one location to another within the same individual. Allograft refers to transferring tissues or organs from a donor to a recipient, with matching criteria to minimize rejection. Other types of transplants may include xenografts (from a different species) or synthetic implants. Each type has its own challenges and benefits, but all aim to improve the recipient's health and quality of life through the transplantation of healthy tissues or organs.</p></p>
