Metallic Type gaskets are made of metal materials like aluminum or steel, providing high strength and durability. Semi-Metallic Type gaskets combine metal with non-metallic materials for a balance of sealing and flexibility. Non-Metallic Type gaskets are made of materials like rubber or silicone for applications where resistance to oil, water, or chemicals is needed.
