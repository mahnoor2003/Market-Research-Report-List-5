<p><strong>The Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brake fluid is classified into four main types based on their chemical composition and performance characteristics: DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 and DOT 4 are glycol-based fluids suitable for most vehicles, with DOT 4 having a higher boiling point for better performance under extreme conditions. DOT 5 is silicon-based and not compatible with glycol-based fluids. DOT 5.1 is a glycol-based fluid with a higher boiling point than DOT 3, providing improved performance and safety for high-performance vehicles. Each type is designed to meet specific needs and requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
