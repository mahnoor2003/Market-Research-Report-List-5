<p><ul><li>Cell-to-Cell Flotation Machine</li><li>Free-Flow Flotation Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Froth flotation machine market can be categorized into two main types: cell-to-cell flotation machine and free-flow flotation machine. In cell-to-cell flotation machines, minerals are separated in a series of cells where each cell acts as a separate unit. On the other hand, in free-flow flotation machines, the mineral slurry flows through a continuous feed-back loop, allowing for efficient separation. Both types of machines are widely used in the mining industry for extracting valuable minerals from ore.</p></p>
