<p><p>RFID and Barcode printers are devices used for printing barcodes and RFID tags onto various items, enabling tracking and identification of products in a supply chain. RFID printers use radio frequency identification technology to create and encode RFID tags, while barcode printers utilize thermal printing technology to print barcodes on labels or tags. These devices are crucial for industries such as retail, manufacturing, healthcare, and logistics for efficient inventory management and tracking.</p><p>The RFID and Barcode Printer market is witnessing steady growth, driven by the increasing adoption of automatic identification and data capture technologies in various industries.
