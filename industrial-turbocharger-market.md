<p><p>An industrial turbocharger is a device that increases the efficiency and power output of an internal combustion engine by forcing extra air into the combustion chamber. This results in better fuel combustion and increased performance of the engine. Industrial turbochargers are widely used in various industries such as automotive, power generation, oil & gas, marine, and aerospace.</p><p>The global industrial turbocharger market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period. The key factors driving this growth include the increasing demand for fuel-efficient and high-performance engines, rising adoption of turbochargers in power generation applications, and stringent emission norms imposed by governments worldwide. Additionally, advancements in turbocharger technology such as the development of electric turbochargers and variable geometry turbochargers are further boosting the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the growing trend towards downsizing of engines in various industries is also driving the demand for industrial turbochargers. Manufacturers are focusing on developing more efficient and compact turbochargers to meet the increasing demand for smaller engine sizes without compromising on performance. These trends are expected to significantly contribute to the growth of the industrial turbocharger market in the coming years.</p></p>
