<p><p>The Bicycle Taillight market consists of two main types: Self Generating Lights and Battery Powered Lights. Self Generating Lights are powered by the energy generated by the movement of the bicycle, eliminating the need for batteries. They are environmentally friendly and require no extra cost for replacement batteries. On the other hand, Battery Powered Lights rely on replaceable batteries to function. While they offer convenience and often come with various lighting modes, they require regular battery replacements which can add to operating costs over time.</p></p>
