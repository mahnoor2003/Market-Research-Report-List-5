<p><p>Half couplings are commonly used in piping systems to connect pipes of different sizes or materials. There are two main types of half couplings in the market: screw thread type and socket welding type. Screw thread type half couplings feature internal or external threads for easy installation and removal, while socket welding type half couplings are welded directly onto the pipe for a secure and leak-proof connection. Both types offer reliable and efficient ways to join pipes in various industrial applications.</p></p>
