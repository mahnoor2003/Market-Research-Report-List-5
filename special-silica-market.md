<p><p>Special silica is available in various types including precipitated silica, fumed silica, fused silica, silica gel, colloidal silica, and others. Precipitated silica is produced by chemical precipitation, while fumed silica is made through flame hydrolysis. Fused silica is created by melting high-purity silica, and silica gel is a porous form used for moisture absorption. Colloidal silica consists of tiny particles suspended in a liquid. The "Others" category encompasses unique forms of silica with specific properties and applications.</p></p>
