<p><ul><li>Pulsed Pump Laser</li><li>Quasi-Continuous Pump Laser</li><li>CW-Pumped Laser</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diode Pumped Lasers are categorized into different types based on their pumping mechanisms. Pulsed Pump Lasers generate short bursts of high-energy laser beams, Quasi-Continuous Pump Lasers produce a continuous output with periodic interruptions, and CW-Pumped Lasers operate continuously. Other types may include end-pumped lasers and side-pumped lasers that utilize different methods for delivering pump energy to the gain medium. Each type of diode pumped laser has its own unique characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
