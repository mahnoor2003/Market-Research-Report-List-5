<p><p>Food ultrasound market can be categorized into two main types: high-frequency low-intensity and low-frequency high-intensity. High-frequency low-intensity ultrasound involves using a higher frequency and lower energy levels to impact food properties such as texture and structure without causing damage. On the other hand, low-frequency high-intensity ultrasound uses lower frequency but higher energy levels to achieve effects like extraction of compounds or microbial inactivation. Both types offer unique benefits and applications in the food industry for processing and preservation.</p></p>
