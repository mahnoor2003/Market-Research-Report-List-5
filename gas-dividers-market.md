<p><p>Vertical Gas Dividers are devices used to divide a gas stream vertically into multiple smaller streams with uniform flow rates. These dividers are commonly used in applications where precise gas distribution is required, such as in laboratories or industrial processes.</p><p>Horizontal Gas Dividers, on the other hand, divide a gas stream horizontally into multiple smaller streams. They are also used in applications where uniform gas distribution is essential, but the direction of flow needs to be horizontal instead of vertical. Both types of gas dividers play a crucial role in ensuring efficient gas distribution in various industries.</p></p>
