<p><p>Peripheral Intravenous Catheters (PIVC) are commonly used medical devices for intravenous therapy. Ported PIVCs have additional ports that allow for additional medication administration or blood withdrawal without the need for multiple needle sticks. Non-Ported PIVCs do not have additional ports and are used for single medication infusions. Both types of PIVCs have different market demands based on healthcare facility requirements and patient conditions, with ported PIVCs being more versatile and non-ported PIVCs being simpler for basic infusion needs.</p></p>
