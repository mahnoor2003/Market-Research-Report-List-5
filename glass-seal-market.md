<p><p>The Glass Seal Market is categorized into two main types based on temperature requirements: Low Temperature Sealing Glass and High Temperature Sealing Glass. Low Temperature Sealing Glass is designed for applications that require frequent resealing at temperatures below 300°C, such as consumer electronics and medical devices. On the other hand, High Temperature Sealing Glass is used in applications that demand superior thermal resistance at temperatures exceeding 300°C, such as in automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors. Each type offers specific properties to cater to different market needs.</p></p>
