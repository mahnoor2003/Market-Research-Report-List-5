<p><p>Smart garage door openers come in three main types: belt drive openers, chain drive openers, and screw drive openers. Belt drive openers operate quietly and smoothly, making them a popular choice for homes with bedrooms above the garage. Chain drive openers are durable and reliable, but may be noisy during operation. Screw drive openers are known for their minimal maintenance requirements and are a good option for those looking for a balance between durability and noise level.</p></p>
