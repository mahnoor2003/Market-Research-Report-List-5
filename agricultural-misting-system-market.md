<p><p>Agricultural misting systems can be categorized into two main types: low pressure misting systems and high pressure misting systems. Low pressure misting systems operate at a lower pressure range, typically between 40-250 psi, and are more suitable for smaller scale applications such as residential gardens or greenhouses. On the other hand, high pressure misting systems operate at a higher pressure range, typically above 800 psi, and are more suitable for larger scale agricultural operations such as commercial farms or nurseries.</p></p>
