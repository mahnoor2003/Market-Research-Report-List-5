The Solar Tower system uses a tall tower surrounded by a field of mirrors to concentrate sunlight and produce heat for power generation. Other CSP technologies include Dish/Engine systems and Linear Fresnel systems, each with unique methods of concentrating solar energy to generate power.</p></p>
