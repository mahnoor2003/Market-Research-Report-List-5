<p><p>GPS antenna modules are essential components of GPS systems, which receive signals from satellites to determine accurate location data. There are two main types of GPS antenna modules in the market - active and passive. Active GPS antenna modules actively amplify and enhance incoming signals, providing better accuracy and performance. On the other hand, passive GPS antenna modules do not require power and simply receive and transmit signals without any amplification. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring precise GPS tracking and location-based services.</p></p>
