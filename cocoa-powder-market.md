<p><ul><li>Natural Cocoa Powder</li><li>Alkalized/Dutch-process Cocoa Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cocoa powder can be classified into two main types: natural cocoa powder and alkalized/Dutch-process cocoa powder. Natural cocoa powder is made from cocoa beans with minimal processing, resulting in a lighter color and acidic taste. In contrast, alkalized/Dutch-process cocoa powder undergoes a process involving alkaline solutions to reduce acidity and create a darker color. Both types are used in various food products, with natural cocoa powder favored for its more intense flavor and alkalized cocoa powder for its smoother taste.</p></p>
