Single Channel PH meters are designed to measure the PH of a single sample at a time, making them ideal for small scale applications. Dual Channel PH meters can monitor two different samples simultaneously, offering increased efficiency. Multi Channel PH meters have the capability to measure multiple samples at once, making them suitable for high-throughput testing and large-scale laboratories. Each type caters to different needs based on the scale of operations.</p></p>
