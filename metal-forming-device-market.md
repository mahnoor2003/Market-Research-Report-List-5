<p><p>The metal forming device market offers various types of equipment tailored for different applications. Tensile forming equipment is used to stretch and shape metal sheets without altering its thickness. Combined tensile and compressive forming equipment performs both stretching and compressing operations on metal sheets. Bending equipment is designed to bend metal sheets into desired shapes, while shearing equipment is used to cut metal sheets into specific sizes. Each type of equipment serves a specific purpose in the metal forming process.</p></p>
