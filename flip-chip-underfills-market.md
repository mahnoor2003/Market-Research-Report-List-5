<p><strong>The Flip Chip Underfills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capillary Underfill Material (CUF)</li><li>No Flow Underfill Material (NUF)</li><li>Molded Underfill Material (MUF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flip chip underfills are used in semiconductor packaging to protect and insulate the delicate components. There are three main types of flip chip underfills: Capillary Underfill Material (CUF) which flows between the chip and substrate by capillary action, No Flow Underfill Material (NUF) which does not flow during curing, and Molded Underfill Material (MUF) which is pre-applied and encapsulates the chip during molding. Each type offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
