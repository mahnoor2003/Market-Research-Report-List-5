<p><p>High Speed Amplifiers are categorized based on the type of power supply they require - Single Power Supply and Dual Power Supply. Single Power Supply amplifiers operate with only one voltage supply, making them simpler to design and use in applications where space and power consumption are considerations. Dual Power Supply amplifiers use two voltage supplies, enabling them to achieve higher performance and better efficiency in applications that require higher voltage swings or greater dynamic range. Both types cater to different needs based on the application requirements.</p></p>
