<p><p>Brass gate valves are available in two main types: solder end and threaded end. Solder end valves are designed to be connected to pipes using soldering techniques, providing a secure and leak-proof connection. Threaded end valves, on the other hand, have threaded ends that can be easily screwed onto pipes without the need for soldering. Both types offer reliable control of fluid flow and are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p></p>
