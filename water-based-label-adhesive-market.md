<p><p>Water-based label adhesive is a type of adhesive used to affix labels to various surfaces such as packaging materials, bottles, and containers. Water-based label adhesive offers strong adhesion, flexibility, and resistance to temperature changes, making it a popular choice in the packaging industry.</p><p>The water-based label adhesive market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for sustainable and eco-friendly labeling solutions. The market is also benefiting from the strict regulations regarding the use of harmful chemicals in adhesives, which is prompting manufacturers to switch to water-based options. Furthermore, the growing adoption of packaged food and beverages is fueling the demand for water-based label adhesive, as manufacturers look for labeling solutions that are safe for food contact.</p><p>The water-based label adhesive market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 9.9% during the forecast period, indicating a positive outlook for the industry.
