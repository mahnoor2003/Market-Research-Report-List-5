<p><strong>The Fire Detection Alarm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Fire Alarm Systems</li><li>Addressable Fire Alarm Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conventional Fire Alarm Systems are basic systems that divide a building into zones, where each zone is monitored by a separate circuit. When a fire is detected, the alarm in that specific zone is activated, helping to identify the location of the fire. </p><p>Addressable Fire Alarm Systems are more advanced, with each device having a unique address on the system. This allows for specific identification of the location of a fire or fault within the building, providing more targeted and efficient responses to emergencies.</p></p>
