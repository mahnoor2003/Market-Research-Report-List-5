<p><p>The External Ventricular Drainage System Market includes various types such as valves, shunts, external drainage systems, and others. Valves are used to control the flow of cerebrospinal fluid, while shunts help divert the fluid from the brain to another part of the body for absorption. External drainage systems are used for temporary drainage of fluid from the brain. Other types of devices may include monitoring systems or programmable valves for customized treatment. Each type serves a specific purpose in managing intracranial pressure and hydrocephalus.</p></p>
