<p><p>The Medium Molecular Weight Epoxy Resin market can be divided into three main types based on viscosity: Low Viscosity Epoxy Resin, Medium Viscosity Epoxy Resin, and High Viscosity Epoxy Resin. Low viscosity epoxy resins are more fluid and easier to apply, making them ideal for casting and laminating applications. Medium viscosity epoxy resins offer a balance between flowability and thickness, commonly used in coatings and adhesives. High viscosity epoxy resins are thick and provide excellent gap filling properties, suitable for structural bonding and construction applications.</p></p>
