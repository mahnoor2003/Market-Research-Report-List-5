<p><p>Multiplex detection immunoassays utilize various techniques to simultaneously detect multiple analytes. Nucleic acid-based techniques employ DNA or RNA probes to detect target molecules. Protein-based multiplex techniques use antibodies to identify multiple proteins. Biosensor-based techniques involve using biosensors to detect analytes by converting biochemical reactions into detectable signals. Each type of market offers unique advantages in terms of sensitivity, specificity, throughput, and ease of use, making them valuable tools in medical diagnostics, drug development, and research applications.</p></p>
