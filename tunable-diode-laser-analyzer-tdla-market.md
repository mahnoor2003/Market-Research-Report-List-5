<p><strong>The Tunable Diode Laser Analyzer (TDLA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Situ TDLA</li><li>Extractive TDLA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tunable Diode Laser Analyzer (TDLA) market includes two main types: In-Situ TDLA and Extractive TDLA. In-Situ TDLA refers to analyzers that measure gas concentrations directly in the process stream or stack, providing real-time data and allowing for immediate action to be taken. On the other hand, Extractive TDLA involves sampling gases from the process stream for analysis in a separate location, offering flexibility in installation and the ability to monitor multiple points with a single analyzer. Both types offer accurate and reliable gas analysis solutions for various industrial applications.</p></p>
