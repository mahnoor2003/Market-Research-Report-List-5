<p><p>Flow enhancers are chemicals designed to improve the flow properties of liquids. There are various types of flow enhancers in the market including viscosity reducer, emulsion breaker, drop coagulant, and others. Viscosity reducers help to decrease the resistance to flow in liquids, emulsion breakers separate emulsions into their individual components, drop coagulants help in the coagulation of suspended particles, while other types of flow enhancers offer specialized functions in improving flow properties. Each type of flow enhancer serves a unique purpose in optimizing fluid flow.</p></p>
