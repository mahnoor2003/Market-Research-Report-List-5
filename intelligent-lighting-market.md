<p><p>Intelligent lighting systems can be categorized into different types based on their functionality. Daylight sensing lighting systems adjust the light intensity based on natural light availability to save energy and enhance comfort. Occupancy sensing systems control lighting based on occupancy to reduce energy wastage in unoccupied areas. Ultrasonic lighting systems use ultrasonic waves to detect motion and adjust lighting accordingly. Other market types include sound sensing systems that adjust lighting based on sound levels, and time scheduling systems that automatically turn off lights at specific times to conserve energy.</p></p>
