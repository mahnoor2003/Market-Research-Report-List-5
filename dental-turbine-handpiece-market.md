<p><p>Dental turbine handpieces are available in two main types: inside exhaust handpieces and outside exhaust handpieces. Inside exhaust handpieces release air and water through the back of the handpiece, while outside exhaust handpieces release air and water through the head of the handpiece. Inside exhaust handpieces are generally quieter and more efficient, while outside exhaust handpieces are easier to clean and maintain. Both types offer distinct advantages and are used based on the preferences of the dentist and the specific treatment being performed.</p></p>
