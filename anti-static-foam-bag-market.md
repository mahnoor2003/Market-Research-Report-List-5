<p><p>Anti-static foam bags are categorized based on the type of polyethylene used in their production. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) foam bags are strong and have a high tensile strength, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Middle Density Polyethylene (MDPE) foam bags offer a balance between strength and flexibility. Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) foam bags are soft and flexible, ideal for cushioning delicate items. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) foam bags provide excellent puncture and tear resistance while remaining lightweight.</p></p>
