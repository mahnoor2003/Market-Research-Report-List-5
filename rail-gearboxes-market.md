<p><strong>The Rail Gearboxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Drive</li><li>Transversal Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rail gearboxes are essential components in rail vehicles for transmitting power from the engine to the wheels. There are two main types of gearboxes in the market; longitudinal drive and transversal drive. Longitudinal drive gearboxes are positioned parallel to the direction of the vehicle, allowing for efficient power transmission in a straight line. On the other hand, transversal drive gearboxes are positioned perpendicular to the vehicle's direction, enabling power transmission for turning and maneuvering. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and reliable operation of rail vehicles.</p></p>
