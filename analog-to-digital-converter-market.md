<p><p>Analog to Digital Converter (ADC) market is segmented into various types such as Display ADC, Delta-sigma ADC, Pipelined ADC, and Dual Slope ADC. Display ADCs convert analog signals into digital signals for display purposes. Delta-sigma ADCs use oversampling and noise shaping techniques for high resolution and accuracy. Pipelined ADCs utilize multiple stages for faster conversion rates. Dual Slope ADCs use a simple integrator circuit to convert analog signals into digital form with high accuracy and low noise. Each type caters to different requirements in the market.</p></p>
