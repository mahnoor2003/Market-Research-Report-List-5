<p><ul><li>Single face brake</li><li>Power off brake</li><li>Particle brake</li><li>Hysteresis power brake</li><li>Multiple disk brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electro-mechanical brake market consists of various types such as single face brake, power off brake, particle brake, hysteresis power brake, and multiple disk brake. Single face brake utilizes electrical current to apply the brake, while power off brake engages automatically when power is shut off. Particle brake uses magnetic particles to control braking force, hysteresis power brake employs eddy currents for braking, and multiple disk brake contains multiple disks for enhanced braking power. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and applications within the electro-mechanical brake market.</p></p>
