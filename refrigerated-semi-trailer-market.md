<p><p>Refrigerated semi-trailers are classified into two main types: single temperature and multi-temperature. Single temperature refrigerated semi-trailers are designed to carry goods that require a consistent temperature throughout the entire trailer. On the other hand, multi-temperature refrigerated semi-trailers feature multiple compartments with individual temperature controls, allowing for the transport of products that require different temperature settings. Both types cater to the diverse needs of the transportation industry, providing flexibility and efficiency in transporting perishable goods.</p></p>
