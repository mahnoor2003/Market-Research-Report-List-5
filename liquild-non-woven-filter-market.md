<p><strong>The Liquild Non-woven Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Meltblown</li><li>Needle Felt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid non-woven filter market can be categorized into two main types: Meltblown and Needle Felt filters. Meltblown filters are made by melting and extruding polymeric materials to create a fine fiber matrix, while Needle Felt filters are created by mechanically interlocking synthetic fibers. Meltblown filters are known for their high filtration efficiency and are commonly used in medical and industrial applications. On the other hand, Needle Felt filters are valued for their durability and ability to handle high temperatures, making them popular in applications like automotive and oil filtration.</p></p>
