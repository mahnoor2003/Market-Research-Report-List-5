<p><p>Industrial trinocular microscopes are available in two main types: inverted type and upright type. Inverted type microscopes are designed for observing specimens from underneath, commonly used in cell culture and metallurgical applications. Upright type microscopes are more traditional, with specimens viewed from above, ideal for routine laboratory use and material analysis. Both types offer trinocular viewing, allowing for simultaneous observation and documentation of specimens through a camera or other imaging device. These microscopes cater to different industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
