<p><p>Polypropylene pipes are commonly categorized into three main types based on their applications and properties. PP-R/RCT pipes are suitable for hot and cold water applications due to their high temperature resistance. PP-H pipes are highly chemical resistant and often used in industrial settings where corrosive materials are present. PP-B pipes are known for their high stiffness and are typically used in underground piping systems for utilities and infrastructure projects. Each type of polypropylene pipe has specific advantages and is selected based on the requirements of the application.</p></p>
