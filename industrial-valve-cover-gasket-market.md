<p><p>Industrial valve cover gaskets are categorized into different types based on their material composition. Non-metallic gaskets are made of materials like rubber or cork, providing flexibility and resistance to chemicals. Semi-metallic gaskets combine the characteristics of both metal and non-metallic gaskets. Metallic gaskets are made entirely of metal, offering high strength and durability. Graphite gaskets are known for their excellent heat and chemical resistance. PTFE gaskets, made of polytetrafluoroethylene, are highly resistant to corrosion and provide a tight seal in high-pressure applications.</p></p>
