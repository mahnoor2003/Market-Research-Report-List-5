<p><p>Mobility software can be categorized into two main types: On-premises and Cloud-Based. On-premises software is installed and operated from the organization's physical location, requiring in-house infrastructure and IT support. On the other hand, Cloud-Based software is accessed over the internet, stored on remote servers, and managed by a third-party provider. Cloud-Based solutions offer greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness compared to on-premises software, making them increasingly popular among businesses looking for mobile workforce management solutions.</p></p>
