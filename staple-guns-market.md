<p><p>Staple guns are commonly categorized into three types based on their operation: manual staple guns, electrical staple guns, and pneumatic staple guns. Manual staple guns are hand-operated and require physical force to drive staples into materials. Electrical staple guns are powered by electricity and offer faster and easier operation compared to manual ones. Pneumatic staple guns use compressed air to drive staples into materials, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different scenarios based on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
