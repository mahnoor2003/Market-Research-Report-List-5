<p><p>Engineered Quartz (E-Quartz) market comprises two main types: Phaneromer Quartz and Cryptocrystalline Quartz. Phaneromer Quartz refers to quartz crystals that are visible to the naked eye, providing a more aesthetic appeal in countertops and other applications. On the other hand, Cryptocrystalline Quartz consists of minuscule quartz particles that are not easily visible, but still provide durability and strength. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market for engineered quartz products.</p></p>
