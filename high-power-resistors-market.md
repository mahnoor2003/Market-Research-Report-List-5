<p><strong>The High Power Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chassis Mounted</li><li>Surface Mounted</li><li>Through-Hole Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High power resistors are classified into three main types based on their mounting method. Chassis mounted resistors are directly mounted on the chassis of a device for efficient heat dissipation. Surface mounted resistors are soldered onto the surface of a printed circuit board, making them ideal for compact electronic devices. Through-hole mounted resistors have leads that are inserted into holes on a PCB and soldered on the opposite side, providing a secure and sturdy connection. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
