<p><p>Body fat measurement market includes various types of technologies such as Bioimpedance Analyzers (BIA), Body Fat Skinfold Calipers, Hydrostatic Weighing (HwD), Air Displacement Plethysmography (ADP), and Dual Emission X-ray Absorptiometry (DEXA). BIA measures the resistance of electrical flow through body tissues, Skinfold Calipers measure skinfold thickness, HwD measures body volume by underwater weighing, ADP measures body volume by air displacement, and DEXA uses x-rays to measure bone density and body composition. Each of these technologies offers unique advantages for accurately measuring body fat percentage.</p></p>
