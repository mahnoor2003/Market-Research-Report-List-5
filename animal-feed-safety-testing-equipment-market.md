<p><p>Animal feed safety testing equipment market consists of various types such as chromatography systems, spectrometry systems, immunoassay systems, flow cytometry systems, and others. Chromatography systems are used to identify and separate compounds in feed samples. Spectrometry systems analyze the composition of samples based on the interaction of light with the sample. Immunoassay systems detect specific compounds in feed using antibodies. Flow cytometry systems analyze and count cells in feed samples. Other systems may include sensors, testing kits, and rapid testing devices.</p></p>
