<p><p>The Recycled Pulp market can be classified into three main types: Mechanical Pulping, Chemical Pulping, and Others. Mechanical Pulping involves grinding wood fibers using mechanical energy, resulting in lower quality but higher yield pulp. Chemical Pulping utilizes chemical solutions to break down wood fibers, producing higher quality pulp suitable for a variety of applications. The Others market includes alternative methods such as deinking and agro-residue pulping, offering additional options for sustainable pulp production. Each type caters to different needs within the recycled pulp industry.</p></p>
