<p><p>Microwave downconverters are used to translate microwave signals from higher frequencies to lower intermediate frequencies for easier processing. Different market types include L-band, Ku-band, Ka-band, K-band, and others. L-band downconverters operate in the 1-2 GHz range, Ku-band in the 12-18 GHz range, Ka-band in the 26.5-40 GHz range, and K-band in the 18-27 GHz range. Each market type caters to specific frequency bands and applications, offering tailored solutions for various communication and radar systems.</p></p>
