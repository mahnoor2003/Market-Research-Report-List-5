<p><strong>The Honeycomb Ceramic Catalyst Carrier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flow-through Carrier</li><li>Wall-flow Carrier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Honeycomb ceramic catalyst carriers are categorized into two main types: Flow-through carrier and Wall-flow carrier. Flow-through carriers allow the exhaust gas to flow through the channels of the carrier, promoting efficient contact with the catalyst coating. On the contrary, wall-flow carriers force the gas to flow through the porous walls of the carrier, providing longer contact time with the catalyst. Both these market types are crucial in industries where emissions control is necessary for environmental compliance.</p></p>
