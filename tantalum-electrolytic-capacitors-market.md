<p><p>Tantalum Electrolytic Capacitors come in three main types: Tantalum Foil Electrolytic Capacitor, Tantalum Capacitor with Porous Anode and Liquid Electrolyte, and Tantalum Capacitor with Porous Anode and Solid Electrolyte. Tantalum Foil Electrolytic Capacitors use tantalum foil as the anode material, while the other two types use a porous tantalum anode.
