Tridymite is a high-temperature form of silica ore, mainly used in ceramics and refractories. Cristobalite is a polymorph of quartz often used in the production of glass and ceramics. Coesite is a rare form of silica ore found in high-pressure environments and has applications in geology and research.</p></p>
