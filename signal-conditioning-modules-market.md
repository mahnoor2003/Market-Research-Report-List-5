Rack-Mounted modules are designed to be mounted onto equipment racks for more stable and secure placement. Modular modules allow for customization and flexibility by allowing different modules to be easily connected together. Standalone modules are self-contained units that do not require additional hardware for operation, making them ideal for applications where simplicity is key.</p></p>
