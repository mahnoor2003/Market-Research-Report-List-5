<p><p>Electric Discharge Machines (EDM) are categorized into two types: Electric Spark Forming Machines and EDM Wire Cutting Machines. Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics for their high precision and ability to work with hard materials like titanium and hardened steel.</p></p>
