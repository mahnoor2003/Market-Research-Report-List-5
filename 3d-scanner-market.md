<p><p>Laser 3D Scanners utilize laser beams to detect the distance to an object and create a 3D model with high precision and accuracy. Structured Light 3D Scanners project light patterns onto an object and use cameras to capture the deformation of these patterns, creating detailed 3D models. Other types of 3D scanners include Contact 3D Scanners, Photogrammetry, and Time-of-Flight scanners, each offering unique capabilities and applications in industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and entertainment.</p></p>
