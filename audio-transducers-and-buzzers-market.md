<p><p>Audio transducers and buzzers are devices that convert electrical signals into sound waves. There are two main types in the market - electromagnetic and piezoelectric transducers. Electromagnetic transducers use a coil and a magnet to produce sound, while piezoelectric transducers use a crystal that vibrates when an electrical signal is applied. Both types have their own advantages and are commonly used in different applications such as alarms, notifications, and communication devices. The market for these transducers continues to grow as the demand for audio solutions increases in various industries.</p></p>
