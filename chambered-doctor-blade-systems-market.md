<p><p>Chambered doctor blade systems are commonly available in two types in the market: single-blade system and dual-blade system. In a single-blade system, there is only one doctor blade that comes into contact with the printing surface. On the other hand, a dual-blade system utilizes two doctor blades for enhanced precision and control over the printing process. Both systems offer efficient ink containment and distribution, with the dual-blade system offering higher performance and versatility compared to the single-blade system.</p></p>
