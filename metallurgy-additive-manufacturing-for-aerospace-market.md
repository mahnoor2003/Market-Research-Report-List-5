<p><p>Metallurgy additive manufacturing for aerospace includes types such as Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM involves using a high-powered laser to selectively fuse metal powder together layer by layer to create 3D parts. EBM, on the other hand, uses an electron beam to melt and fuse metal powder together in a vacuum environment. Both technologies offer high precision and complexity in manufacturing aerospace components, making them popular choices in the industry.</p></p>
