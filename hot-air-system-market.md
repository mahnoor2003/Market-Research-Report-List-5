<p><ul><li>Central Heat</li><li>Direct Heat</li><li>Hybrid Heating</li><li>Ground Source Heat Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot air systems are commonly classified into four types: central heat systems, direct heat systems, hybrid heating systems, and ground source heat pump systems. Central heat systems distribute heated air throughout a building via ductwork and vents. Direct heat systems utilize individual units to heat specific areas directly. Hybrid heating systems combine elements of both central heat and direct heat systems for increased efficiency. Ground source heat pump systems use ground heat to warm air before distributing it throughout a building, providing a more sustainable heating option.</p></p>
