<p><p>Thermoformed plastic market includes various types such as PP, PE, ABS, PVC, and others. PP (polypropylene) is known for its durability and chemical resistance. PE (polyethylene) is versatile and commonly used for packaging and containers. ABS (acrylonitrile butadiene styrene) is strong and impact resistant. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is rigid and cost-effective. Other types of thermoformed plastics include PET, polycarbonate, and acrylic, each with their unique properties and applications in industries like automotive, packaging, and construction.</p></p>
