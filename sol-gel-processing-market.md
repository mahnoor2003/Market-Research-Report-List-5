<p><p>Sol-Gel Processing Market Types include rotational coating, immersion coating, roll coating, and others. Rotational coating involves revolving the substrate to deposit the sol-gel material evenly. Immersion coating submerges the substrate into the sol-gel solution for uniform coating. Roll coating uses rollers to apply the sol-gel material onto the substrate. Other methods may include spray coating, dip coating, or spin coating. Each of these market types offers specific advantages and applications for sol-gel processing in various industries.</p></p>
