<p><p>Vertical refrigerators for laboratories come in three main types: gas compression, gas absorption, and semiconductor refrigerators. Gas compression refrigerators use a pump to compress and cool the gas refrigerant. Gas absorption refrigerators use a heat source to drive the cooling process. Semiconductor refrigerators utilize the Peltier effect to create a temperature difference between two electrical junctions. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the laboratory setting.</p></p>
