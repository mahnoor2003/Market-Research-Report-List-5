<p><ul><li>Inrush Current Limitation</li><li>Antenna Matching</li><li>Snubbing Networks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic resistors are commonly used in various applications such as inrush current limitation, antenna matching, and snubbing networks. In inrush current limitation, ceramic resistors help control the amount of current flowing into a system during startup, preventing damage to components. Antenna matching involves adjusting impedance for optimal signal transmission, which ceramic resistors facilitate. Snubbing networks use ceramic resistors to suppress voltage spikes and protect sensitive electronic devices. Overall, ceramic resistors play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and reliability of electronic systems.</p></p>
