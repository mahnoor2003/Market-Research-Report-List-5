<p><p>Desktop 3D Printers market is segmented into three main types based on technology: Material Extrusion Technology, Light Polymerization Technology, and Other Technology. Material Extrusion Technology involves melting and pushing out the material through a nozzle to create the desired object. Light Polymerization Technology uses light to solidify liquid resin layer by layer. Other Technology includes techniques such as powder bed fusion or jetting. Each type of technology offers different benefits and capabilities for creating 3D printed objects on a desktop scale.</p></p>
