<p><p>Wind power transmission equipment market consists of various types such as gearboxes, couplings, and others. Gearboxes are crucial components in wind turbines that translate the low-speed rotation of the blades into higher-speed rotation for generating electricity. Couplings are used to connect different parts of the transmission system while others include components like shafts, bearings, and sensors. These equipment are essential for the proper functioning of wind turbines, enabling the efficient transmission of power from the blades to the generator.</p></p>
