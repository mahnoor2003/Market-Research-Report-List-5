There are three main types of torque limiting clutches in the market - friction type, ball type, and others. Friction type clutches rely on friction between surfaces to limit torque, while ball type clutches utilize rolling elements to achieve the same effect. Other types may include magnetic or shear pin clutches, each offering unique benefits in various applications.</p></p>
