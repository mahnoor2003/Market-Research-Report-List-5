<p><p>The hemodialysis device market includes three main types: hemodialysis machines, water treatment systems, and dialyzers. Hemodialysis machines are used to remove waste and excess fluid from the blood of patients with kidney failure. Water treatment systems ensure that the water used in the dialysis process is safe and clean. Dialyzers act as filters in the machine, removing impurities and toxins from the blood. These three components work together to provide life-saving treatment for patients with kidney disease.</p></p>
