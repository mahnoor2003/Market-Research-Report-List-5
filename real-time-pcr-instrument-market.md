<p><strong>The Real-time PCR Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-step Real Time PCR</li><li>Two-step Real Time PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Real-time PCR instruments are used for quantitative analysis of DNA samples in a rapid and accurate manner. One-step Real Time PCR involves both reverse transcription and PCR amplification in a single tube, simplifying the process and reducing the risk of contamination. Two-step Real Time PCR separates the reverse transcription and PCR amplification steps into two tubes, allowing for more flexibility and optimization of each step. Both types of Real-time PCR instruments have their own advantages and are widely used in research, clinical diagnostics, and other applications.</p></p>
