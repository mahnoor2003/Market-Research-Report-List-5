<p><p>Surface profile measurement market includes two main types: stylus surface profiler and non-contact profiler. Stylus surface profiler utilizes a probe that physically touches the surface to measure the profile. On the other hand, non-contact profiler uses advanced technologies like laser or optical sensors to measure the surface profile without touching the surface. Both types have their own advantages and applications based on the specific requirements of the industry or application.</p></p>
