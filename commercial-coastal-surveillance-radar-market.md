<p><p>Commercial Coastal Surveillance Radar Market consists of two main types of radars: X-Band Radar and S-Band Radar. X-Band Radar operates at higher frequencies, providing high resolution and accuracy for short-range surveillance. It is often used for port security and small vessel detection. On the other hand, S-Band Radar operates at lower frequencies, offering longer detection range and better performance in adverse weather conditions. It is commonly used for large area surveillance and monitoring of maritime activities in coastal regions. Both types of radars play a crucial role in ensuring maritime security and safety.</p></p>
