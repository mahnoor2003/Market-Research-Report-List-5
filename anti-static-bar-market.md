<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Inductive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti Static Bar market is divided into two main types: Contact Type and Inductive Type. Contact Type Anti Static Bars make direct contact with the material being treated to neutralize static charges, while Inductive Type Anti Static Bars emit ions into the surrounding area to counteract static electricity. Both types are used in various industries where static electricity can pose a risk, such as printing, packaging, and electronics manufacturing. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications based on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
