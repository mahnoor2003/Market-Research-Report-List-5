<p><p>Stent-assisted coiling is a minimally invasive procedure used to treat brain aneurysms. The market includes various types of devices such as self-expandable stents, liquid embolic devices, flow-diversion devices, and embolic coils. Self-expandable stents are used to support the coil inside the aneurysm. Liquid embolic devices are injectable materials that solidify within the aneurysm to prevent blood flow. Flow-diversion devices redirect blood flow away from the aneurysm. Embolic coils are coils inserted into the aneurysm to block blood flow and promote clotting.</p></p>
