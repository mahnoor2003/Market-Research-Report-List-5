<p><p>Tuberculosis testing market offers various types of diagnostics methods. Smear microscopy involves examining sputum samples under a microscope. Nucleic acid testing detects TB DNA in samples. Radiography uses X-rays to identify TB-related abnormalities in the lungs. Culture based tests grow bacteria from samples to confirm TB. Drug susceptibility testing determines the effectiveness of TB drugs. Other methods include serological tests and interferon-gamma release assays. These tests play a crucial role in the early detection and treatment of tuberculosis.</p></p>
