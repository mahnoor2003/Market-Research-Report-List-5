<p><strong>The Distributed Antenna System (DAS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active DAS</li><li>Passive DAS</li><li>Hybrid DAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Antenna System (DAS) market includes three main types: Active DAS, Passive DAS, and Hybrid DAS. </p><p>Active DAS uses amplifiers to distribute signals through the system, making it suitable for large, high-traffic areas. Passive DAS relies on coaxial cables to distribute signals without the need for amplification, making it a cost-effective option for smaller spaces. </p><p>Hybrid DAS combines elements of both Active and Passive DAS systems, offering a flexible and scalable solution for a variety of indoor and outdoor environments.</p></p>
