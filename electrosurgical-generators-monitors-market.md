<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Generator</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator</li><li>Vessel Sealing Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrosurgical generators are devices used in surgeries to deliver high-frequency electrical currents to cut, coagulate, or seal tissues. There are three main types of electrosurgical generators: Monopole generators, which use a single active electrode; Bipolar generators, which use two electrodes to target a specific area; and Vessel sealing generators, which are specifically designed for sealing blood vessels during procedures. Each type of generator offers unique advantages and is used in different surgical scenarios based on the specific needs of the procedure.</p></p>
