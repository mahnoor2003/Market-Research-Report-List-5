<p><p>Full Colour LED Displays are categorized into two main types: Surface Mount Device (SMD) and Chip on Board (COB). SMD LEDs are widely used for their high resolution and brightness, suitable for applications requiring detailed images and videos. On the other hand, COB LEDs offer better heat dissipation and are ideal for larger displays requiring higher power outputs. Both types cater to various market needs, providing versatility and flexibility in creating vibrant and dynamic LED displays.</p></p>
