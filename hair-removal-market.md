<p><p>Hair removal market is dominated by three main types of devices: Energy Based Devices, Laser Based Devices, and IPL Devices. Energy based devices use heat or sound energy to damage hair follicles. Laser based devices target melanin in hair follicles to inhibit growth. IPL devices use broad spectrum light to target melanin, similar to lasers but less concentrated. Each type of device offers different benefits and results, depending on factors such as skin tone and hair color.</p></p>
