<p><strong>The RFMicrowave Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire Wound Type</li><li>Film Type</li><li>Multilayer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF/Microwave inductors are classified into three main types based on their construction: wire wound type, film type, and multilayer type. Wire wound inductors are made by winding a wire around a core material, offering good performance at high frequencies. Film inductors are made by depositing a film of conductive material on a substrate, providing high Q factor and stability. Multilayer inductors are constructed by stacking multiple layers of conductive material, offering high inductance values in a compact form factor. Each type is suited for specific applications based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
