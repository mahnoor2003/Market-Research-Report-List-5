<p><strong>The Aviation Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PNR headsets</li><li>ANR headsets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aviation headsets can be classified into Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) headsets and Active Noise Reduction (ANR) headsets based on their noise-cancelling capabilities. PNR headsets utilize soundproofing materials to block out ambient noise, while ANR headsets use advanced technology to actively cancel out unwanted noise. Both types offer distinct advantages and cater to different preferences and budgets within the aviation industry. PNR headsets are known for their affordability and durability, while ANR headsets provide superior noise reduction for a more comfortable and immersive flying experience.</p></p>
