<p><p>Fixed crash barrier systems are categorized into different types based on their design and material composition. Rigid barriers are made of concrete or steel and provide maximum protection but can cause severe damage to vehicles upon impact. Semi-rigid barriers have some flexibility and absorb some of the impact energy. Flexible barriers, such as cable barriers, are designed to deflect vehicles and reduce the impact force. Other types of barriers may include hybrid systems or innovative designs that offer unique benefits for specific applications.</p></p>
