<p><ul><li>Videonystagmography (VNG) System</li><li>Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potential (VEMP) System</li><li>Rotary Chair</li><li>Computerized Dynamic Posturography</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Videonystagmography (VNG) System is a type of vestibular testing equipment that allows for detailed evaluation of eye movements to assess balance and equilibrium. Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potential (VEMP) System is used to measure the function of the otolithic organs in the inner ear. Rotary Chair is a device that helps diagnose disorders affecting the vestibular system by measuring eye movements in response to rotational stimuli. Computerized Dynamic Posturography assesses postural stability and balance control. Other types of equipment include caloric testing devices and vestibular autorotation testing systems.</p></p>
