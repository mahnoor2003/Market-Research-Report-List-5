<p><p>The Pneumatic Rolling Motor market can be classified into two main types based on the torque capabilities: Torque Controlled and Stall Torque. Torque Controlled motors are designed to operate within a specified torque range, allowing for precise control over the output. On the other hand, Stall Torque motors are able to deliver maximum torque output at zero speed, making them ideal for applications requiring high starting torque. Both types have their own advantages and are suited for different industrial applications.</p></p>
