<p><ul><li>Mechanical Coalescers</li><li>Electrostatic Coalescers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coalescers are utilized in various industries to separate liquid droplets from gas streams. Mechanical coalescers rely on physical barriers to capture and merge liquid droplets, while electrostatic coalescers utilize electric charges to attract droplets and combine them. Both types of coalescers are designed to improve the efficiency of separation processes and reduce the amount of liquid contaminants in gas streams.  These technologies play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and purity of gases in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and power generation.</p></p>
