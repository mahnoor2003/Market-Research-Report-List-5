<p><strong>The Pelvis External Fixation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubular</li><li>Circular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pelvis External Fixation System market consists of two main types: Tubular and Circular. Tubular external fixators are cylindrical in shape and are typically used for simple fractures or non-union cases. Circular external fixators, on the other hand, consist of multiple rings connected by rods and are more versatile, allowing for adjustments in multiple planes. Both types of external fixators provide stabilization and support for pelvic fractures, but circular fixators offer a higher level of customization and adaptability.</p></p>
